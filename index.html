Research

I have published research (as the first author) in the area of deep neural network stability (KDD '2023), which provides intriguing insights into the Lipschitz properties of deep networks. Currently, I am interning at Zhejiang Lab, where my work revolves around accelerating large model training. I find the convergence of research theory and practical industrial scenarios to be highly intriguing, as it enables a more holistic understanding and practical application of the concepts I investigate.

I am sincerely thankful to all the exceptional co-authors who have made valuable contributions to my research journey.
